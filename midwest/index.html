Durable Sewn Solutions for Every Industry

At Midwest Textile Manufacturing, we provide versatile contract sewing services in the USA for industries where quality and reliability matter most. From soft sided cases, custom sewn bags, and industrial carrying solutions to medical soft goods, straps, harnesses, and upholstered products, our team transforms customer concepts into durable sewn solutions. Starting with you prototype or full-scale production, we adapt and innovate to meet your exact requirements — no two projects are ever alike.

Based in Green Bay, Wisconsin, our people are our greatest strength. Skilled sewing operators, experienced production teams, and innovative engineers work together to deliver products that exceed expectations. With decades of expertise as an industrial sewing manufacturer serving the medical, industrial, tool, and defense markets, we bring a broad perspective to every project. For government and military customers, we proudly offer Berry Amendment compliant tactical gear, bags, and soft goods to ensure full compliance and mission readiness.

For over 30 years, customers have trusted us for our custom sewing services, quality craftsmanship, and dependable delivery. We understand our work is often a critical link in your supply chain, and we take that responsibility seriously. Whether it’s a rugged soft sided case, a precision-fit tactical bag, or a specialized OEM sewn component, our passion and urgency drive us to deliver sewn products you can count on — every time.
